Iteration4v2

Keeps finding ways to divide a specified number by 3. Outputs the amount of steps taken

If a number is divisible by 3, the number is divided by 3
If a number is not divisible by 3, a check is made to see whether its successor is divisible by 3
If the successor is not divisible by 3, it divides its predecessor by 3
Once the number hits 1, the amount of steps taken is outputted

This is not the solution to Iteration4. For that, please look at "Iteration4v2"

Outputs the result of numbers when they are divided by 3 to the console, as well as the amount of iterations it takes

Loops through numbers 1 to a user specified number. If a number is divisible by 3, the result of the number divided by 3 will be printed
If the number is not divisible by 3, the number will be incremented by 1 until it is.
The result will then be printed to the console.
